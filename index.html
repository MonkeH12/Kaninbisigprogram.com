KANIN-BISIG PROGRAM

This program facilitates the financial donations that can give farmers the much-needed resources they need to improve their quality of life. Donations may additionally provide farmers access to training and educational opportunities that can improve their expertise. They may learn about sustainable practices, keep up with the most recent farming methods, and adjust to shifting weather patterns as a result of this. Donations might also go toward programs that guarantee the health and productivity of farmers by offering healthcare services. Additionally, by giving farmers emergency relief and supporting in their recuperation from crop losses, donations might lessen the consequences of natural disasters like floods or droughts. Donations can help farmers become more empowered overall, giving them a bigger say in how policies are made and the ability to fight for their rights.
